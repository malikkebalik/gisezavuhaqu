Bart Millard

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Bart Millard is a singer and songwriter who is best known as the frontman of the band MercyMe. Likewise, he also has a solo career releasing two solo albums. He has received a solo Grammy nomination in Best Southern, Country, or Bluegrass Gospel Album.

What is the net worth of Bart Millard?

According to sources, Bart Millard  has a net worth of over $6 million as of 2022, which he has amassed through a successful career in the music industry. He’s been working in the sector since 2004, and his celebrity has led to other opportunities. As he progresses in his career, his net worth is projected to rise.

Childhood and Early age

He was born in 1972 in Greenville, Texas. He celebrates his birthday on December 1, which makes his age 48. Likewise, he holds American nationality and comes from White ethnicity.

Professional life and career

Brat was inspired to start a full-time musical after a trip to Switzerland with his church’s youth group worship band. He remains as one of the members of the band MercyMe. The band has released six independent, nine studios, two exclusives, two Christmas, and one compilation album. Likewise, he also embarked on a solo musical career and released the album Hymned No.1 in 2005.

Besides, he also rereleased Hymned in 2008. Moreover, Bart has also been featured as a vocalist on “I See You,” a single by Third Day and Steven Curtis Chapman, and a backing vocalist on Phil Wickham’s 2009 single “Safe.”

Relationship status of Bart Millard

Bart has been long time married to his wife, Shannon Millard. The singer has not shared many details of his life. Regardless, they met when Shannon was in 8th grade, and Bart was on the school’s football team. The pair tied the knot in 1997 and has been together ever since. Despite being married for over two decades, Bart does not forget to appreciate his wife on social media. On May 10, 2021, the singer posted a picture with his wife writing,

“She’s everything I’ve hoped for. She’s everything I need.”

Likewise, the married couple shares a huge family with five children: Sam, Gracie, Charlie, Sophie, and Miles.

The singer has featured his family of seven many times on his social media. Bart and his family reside in Greenville, Texas.

Family, Parents and Siblings

Bart was born to his parents, Arthur Wesley Millard Jr. and Adele Millard. He lost his father in 1991, during Bart’s first year of college. He has featured his mother a few times on his social media.

Likewise, he also has a brother named Stephen Millard.
