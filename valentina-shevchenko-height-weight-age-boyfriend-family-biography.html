Personal Trainer

Valentina Shevchenko trains under the watchful eyes of coach Pavel Fedotov. When she is training for a fight, she usually begins early in the morning and the training gets divided into 2-3 sessions a day. The intensity of the training increases each day as she gets closer to the fight.

During her fight training sessions, she pays special attention to her striking and grappling techniques. She does additional conditioning outdoors during the afternoon which gradually becomes more grueling. On a normal day, she practices sambo, judo, wrestling, boxing, taekwondo, and does gym workout.

As for her diet, she maintains a clean diet throughout, focusing on healthy and nutritious food.

Valentina Shevchenko Facts

  • She started her Taekwondo training when she was 5 years old as it was a family tradition.
  • Her mother, who was also a martial artist, has been Valentina’s inspiration since her young age.

  • She entered her first competition at the age of 7.

  • She has achieved a lot in Muay Thai /K-1 with 56 wins against 2 losses with a combined professional record of 67 wins and 3 losses by 2017.

  • She can speak Russian and Spanish.
  • She has beaten Joanna Jedrzejczyk, UFC’s longtime female Strawweight Champion, many times.
