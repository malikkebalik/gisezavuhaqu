Who Is Ron McGovney?

Ron McGovney, born Ronald J. McGovney is a semi-retired American musician widely recognized as an original bass guitarist in the heavy metal band Metallica in the 1980s.

Furthermore, McGovney formed his first band in June 1981 with his childhood friends Hetfield and Hugh Tanner. They were later joined by guitarist Troy James and drummer Jim Mulligan.
