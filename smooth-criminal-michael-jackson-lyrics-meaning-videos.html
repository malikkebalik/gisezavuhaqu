Overall Meaning

"Smooth Criminal" is a song by American singer and songwriter Michael Jackson. This song tells a story of a woman named Annie who gets attacked by a smooth criminal who came into her apartment. The song portrays an intense and violent scene that includes a crescendo sound and bloodstains on the carpet. Annie tries to escape, but she is unsuccessful, and she dies. Michael Jackson asks if Annie is okay, even though it is evident that she is not. The song ends with Michael asking the audience to leave the room.


The song has been interpreted in different ways, and some people believe that it is a commentary on the state of the world and how it can be a dangerous place for women. The mention of "Mouth to mouth resuscitation" and "sounding heartbeats, intimidation" may also suggest that the attacker tried to revive Annie after the attack. This interpretation could be seen as a reflection of how abusers often rely on their victims' dependence on them to maintain control.
