Fortunately, solving the Shroud Hearth Barrow’s locked door can be found within the town of Ivalstead, specifically in the Inn of Villemir, but first, players must venture into the Norse Tomb in search of the items. An ancient crypt, the Shroud Hearth Tomb is filled with undead enemies such as skeletons and wight variants. Therefore, it would be wise for players to prepare accordingly before entering a dungeon. Wights, in particular, are vulnerable to any silver weapon, which will deal 1.5 times the damage. Fire spells do no extra damage to wights, but are undoubtedly effective against undead enemies.On the other hand, low-level players can recruit the skyline Followers accompany them into dangerous catacombs.
