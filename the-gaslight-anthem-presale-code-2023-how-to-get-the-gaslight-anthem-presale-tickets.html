The Gaslight Anthem, a rock band from New Jersey, USA, formed in 2004, comprises of Brian Fallon (lead vocals, rhythm guitar), Alex Rosamilia (lead guitar, backing vocals), Alex Levine (bass guitar, backing vocals), and Benny Horowitz (drums, percussion).

They have garnered critical acclaim for their five studio albums, featuring a blend of punk rock, heartland rock, and folk influences. The band’s extensive tours across the US and Europe, alongside performances at major festivals like Coachella and Lollapalooza, have firmly established them as a powerhouse in the rock music scene.
