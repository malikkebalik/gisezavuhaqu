By going swimming with her canine companion Marley, Millie Bobby Brown has taken advantage of the dog days of summer.

Although he doesn’t frequently appear on the actress’ social media, Marley is her golden lab.

Fans were fortunate to see Brown holding him in her arms in this uncommon picture. The actress, who was beaming from ear to ear and unveiled a stylish new short hairstyle, appeared to be having a fantastic time this summer.
