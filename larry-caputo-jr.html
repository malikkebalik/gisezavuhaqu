Who is Larry Caputo Jr?

Self-described as a “middle” Long Island Medium, a reality show hosted by Theresa Caputo catapulted her to fame. She used her power to communicate with the dead to help others reunite with their loved ones. From 2011 through 2019, the show focused on Theresa’s efforts to strike a work-life balance.

In addition to her readings and spiritual skills, Long Island Medium gave glimpses of her personal life. Theresa was married to Larry Caputo from 1989 to 2018, and they had two children, Victoria Caputo and Larry Caputo Jr. Here’s all we know about Caputo Jr. now that he’s an adult.

Early life

Throughout 14 seasons, Long Island Medium proved that the Caputo family had become acclimated to living with her mediumship. Caputo Jr. told TLC that his mother’s mediumship was nothing out of the ordinary for them because they had grown up seeing it. His pals were indifferent about his mother’s ability; it came naturally to him as well as it did to them. They never made him feel unusual, even if they felt he was odd. Theresa would even give them dating advice and college recommendations.

Regardless, the 29-year-old expressed his pride in his mother and the happiness she offered to people via her readings. When questioned if his mother ever offered him messages from the afterlife, he explained that Theresa only provided him motherly guidance and never gave him medium instruction.

Victoria, his sister, also revealed insights about their childhood. Despite being surrounded by cameras, she highlighted how unremarkable their upbringing had been. Like any other kid, they went to school, hung out with friends, played sports, and spent time with their families. She continued, “The show disclosed everything that was going on behind the scenes.” They were like any other family: boisterous, amusing, expressive, and a little insane.
