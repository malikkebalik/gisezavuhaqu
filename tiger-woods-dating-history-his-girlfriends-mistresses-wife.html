Tiger Woods' dating history: His girlfriends, mistresses, wife

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The world has rallied around Tiger Woods following his severe car accident in February 2021. In response to the terrifying incident, the golfer’s exes, including Lindsey Vonn and Rachel Uchitel, have offered their support.

See Woods’ relationship history below.

Woods married Elin Nordegren in 2004. The pair have two children together, daughter Sam and son Charlie. Their union crumbled as a result of Woods’ infidelity in late 2009, and their divorce was finalized a year later.

Rachel Uchitel was at the center of Woods’ cheating scandal in 2009, and, more recently, spoke about the affair in the 2021 HBO documentary, “Tiger.”

“I really took the opportunity to be able to clear up any misconceptions that people had about … who I am and the situation because people use me as sort of a brand for … the other woman,” Uchitel said in an interview with Page Six.

Jamie Jungers

Jamie Jungers also claimed she and Woods had an affair. In late 2009, Jungers spoke about the alleged tryst in an interview with the “Today” show, stating they met in 2005 at a nightclub in Las Vegas.

“I didn’t even get a birthday card,” Jungers said during the interview. “I got nothing out of this relationship but a broken heart.”

Cori Rist

Cori Rist, a former lingerie model, claimed she met Woods in 2006 at a nightclub. The two were involved for about six months.

Jaimee Grubbs

A cocktail waitress, Jaimee Grubs claimed she had an affair with Woods that lasted for over two years.

Following Woods’ cheating scandal, the golfer found love again with Olympic skier Lindsey Vonn. The couple, who confirmed their relationship in 2013, dated for nearly three years.

“I will always cherish the memories that we’ve created together. Unfortunately, we both lead incredibly hectic lives that force us to spend a majority of our time apart,” Vonn posted to social media in May 2015.

Kristin Smith

Woods dated Kristin Smith from 2015 to 2017. In April 2018, it was reported Smith was in arbitration with Woods over a non-disclosure agreement, according to TMZ.

Smith is said to have ended the relationship after suspecting Woods had been unfaithful.

First linked in 2017, Woods has been dating Erica Herman for over three years. She has joined Woods at golf tournaments, including the 2018 Tour Championship, where he sealed the victory with a kiss from Herman.

In December 2020, Herman joined Nordegren to watch Woods and his young son participate in the PNC Championship.

TMZ reported in March 2023 that Herman was taking Woods to court in hopes of nullifying a non-disclosure agreement the golfer allegedly made her sign in the early days of their six-year romance.

She claims the NDA should not be imposed in light of the Speak Out Act, which, according to Congress, “Prohibits the judicial enforceability of a nondisclosure clause or nondisparagement clause agreed to before a dispute arises involving sexual assault or sexual harassment in violation of federal, tribal, or state law.”

Herman and Woods were most recently photographed at the U.S. Open tennis tournament in August 2022.
