Who is Dave Mustaine?

Dave Mustaine is an American singer, songwriter, guitarist, actor, and author.

He is best known as the co-founder, lead vocalist, guitarist, and primary songwriter of Megadeth and the original lead guitarist of Metallica.

Dave Mustaine: Age(61), Parents, Siblings, Family

He was born on September 13, 1961, in La Mesa, California, USA. His full name is David Scott Mustaine and he is currently 61 years old.

He was born to his mother Emily Mustaine and his father John Mustaine. Also, he has a sister named Michelle Mustaine.

Dave holds American citizenship but his ethnicity is a mix of English, German, Irish, Welsh, Scottish, remote French, Dutch, and Ashkenazi Jewish.

Dave Mustaine: Professional Life and Career

Dave began his career with the band ‘Panic’ in 1978.

The band included Bob Evans on bass, Tom Quecke on rhythm guitar, Mike Leftwych on drums, Pat Voeks as a vocalist, and Dave Mustaine as the lead guitarist.

Unfortunately, Mike Leftwych and a soundman were killed in a car crash and the band began separating in 1981 and also rhythm guitarist Tom Quecke also passed away.

Then, in the same year, he joined Metallica as the lead guitarist, and his membership with the band ended in 1983.

However, he was released early from the band because he was struggling with alcohol and drug abuse, overly aggressive behavior, and personality clashes.

After he left the band, in April 1983, he founded the band ‘Fallen Angels’ alongside Matt Kisselstein, Dave Ellefson, Greg Handevidt, Robbie McKinney, and Lawrence Renna (‘Lor’ Kane).

But, the band was later renamed to ‘Megadeath’.

The band released several albums like ‘Killing Is My Business… and Business Is Good!’, ‘So Far, So Good… So What!’, ‘The System Has Failed’, ‘Cryptic Writings’, ‘Endgame’, and many more.
