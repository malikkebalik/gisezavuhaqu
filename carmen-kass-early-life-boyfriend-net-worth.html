Carmen Kass : Early Life, Boyfriend &amp; Net Worth

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Carmen Kass is an Estonian model and actress who has won the heart of her fans in the runway. The model rose into fame when her face has featured for Gucci, Calvin Klein, and Dior. Moreover, in 2002, Carmen was the second-highest-paid model in the world. Aside from modeling, she is an actress, political candidate, and an entrepreneur.

Carmen Kass is a perfect example of beauty with brains. She was president of the Estonian Chess Federation for eight consecutive years and conducted a campaign for Tallinn to host 2008 Chess Olympics. So, before we begin a discussion of the model’s age, height, husband and net worth, here are some quick facts about her.

Early Life and Education

Carmen Kass was born in Tallinn to her mother Koidu Poder and father, Viktor Kass. The model has got keen on chess from her father as he was a chess teacher in Polva.

She was the youngest member in her family with elder sister Victoria and brother Kutty. Her family belongs to white ethnicity, but we don’t have any information regarding her religion.

Carmen Kass Age, and Body Measurement

The gorgeous diva was born on 14 September 1978 and is 40 years old now. The blonde model has a pair of green eyes. She is 5 feet 10 inches tall with body measurement 32-23-34 inches which is a perfect combo to be a model. The September born girl belongs to Virgo.

Career in Modeling

Carmen Kass commenced her career at an early age of 14. A model scout from Milan caught an eye on the model when she was shopping in the supermarket. But her modeling career did not go as expected; so she left Milan.

Despite, she did not give up on her passion and moved to the city of fashion again. Consequently, she did a marvelous work on the cover page of American, British, French, and Italian Vogue. Anna Wintour, who was the editor-in-chief of Vogue at that time, played a vital role to make her career.

Carmen has worked for luxurious designers such as Marc Jacobs, Calvin Klein, Dolce & Gabbana, Donna Karan, and Ralph Lauren. Similarly, she has stood out for many famous brands such as Fendi, Gucci, Versace, Valentino, Narciso Rodriguez, and General Motors.

Furthermore, the blonde model also did the ad campaigns for Gap, Revlon, Sephora, and Christian Dior perfume. Likewise, this model initiated her career in Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show from 1999 to 2003 and again in 2008.
