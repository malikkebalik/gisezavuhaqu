Is David Licauco Christian- Religion And Ethnicity

David Licauco follows the Christian religion. He came from Chinese Filipino ethnicity.

He attended the Chinese-Filipino school Grace Christian College in Quezon City throughout his formative years.

When he was 18, Licauco competed and finished first runner-up in the Mr. Chinatown 2014 pageant before starting his acting career.

Before making his acting debut in a movie, David made a living as a model and basketball player. He represents the local clothing Company Bench.

He appeared in the 2014 movie The Amazing Praybeyt Benjamin with Vice Ganda. In 2015, he had a guest appearance in the Flordeliza episode of the ABS-CBN television program.

David Licauco Parents: Jolan Alexander And Eden Sy Licauco

He was born to Jolan Alexander and Eden Sy Licauco and is the second oldest of his siblings.  His sibling group includes Elan Alyssandra, Jean Arianne May, and James Daniel.

He is a direct descendant of the prominent parapsychologist, author, lecturer, newspaper columnist, hypnotherapist, and consultant Jaime Licauco.

He started his modeling career in 2014 after placing as Mr. and Ms. Chinatown’s first runner-up.

Licauco earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a Major in Computer Applications from De La Salle College after completing his high school studies at Grace Christian College.

According to his Instagram posts, he and his family go on vacation frequently. He has traveled to several places, including Japan, South Korea, the UAE, and the U.S.

His favorite vacation destinations, though, are Hong Kong and Australia because he has happy memories there with his family.

He frequently visits Tagaytay while traveling locally. He was found by a talent scout while on holiday in Boracay.

David Licauco Net Worth

The estimated range of David Licauco’s net worth or income is $1 million to $5 million. His major profession as a TV Actor has brought him a significant amount of cash.

In the 2017 television fantasy series Mulawin vs. Ravena, Licauco portrayed the main character.

Once he was cast as Malik, Licauco gained notoriety, which boosted his popularity. He thus received roles in the television programs TODA One I Love and Kapag Nahati ang Puso shortly after.

In 2021, Licauco made his television acting debut in the GMA Network’s romance comedy Heartful Café.

It debuted on April 26, 2021, according to the Telebabad timetable for the network. Licauco made his acting debut in the 2022 film Maria Clara at Ibarra.

He portrayed Fidel de los Reyes, the snarky friend of Crisostomo Ibarra, played by Dennis Trillo, and the potential love interest of Klay Infantes, played by Barbie Forteza.

Many media publications referred to his performance as that of the “New Philippine Superstar, not for his boy-next-door looks, but on-point acting skills.”
