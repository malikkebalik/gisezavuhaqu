The Meaning Behind the Song:

From the lyrics, it is clear that the song “Milly Rock” by LAY OFF is exploring themes of financial struggle, the pursuit of wealth, and the importance of perseverance. The phrase “milly rock” refers to a popular dance move that gained popularity in the hip-hop community. However, in this context, it symbolizes the struggle of balancing financial hardships while striving for success.

The line “Парень просто broke” emphasizes the artist’s acknowledgment of their current financial state. Being “broke” signifies their humble beginnings and serves as a motivation to work towards a better future.

The verse delves deeper into the struggle for financial stability, with lines such as “Что бы выжить нужно делать много денег” (To survive, you need to make a lot of money) and “Чтобы делать много денег нужно только время” (To make a lot of money, you only need time). These lyrics highlight the artist’s understanding that financial success requires hard work, dedication, and patience.

Furthermore, the chorus emphasizes the connection between money and communication. “Это money talk” (This is money talk) suggests that financial success can open doors and create opportunities for networking and influence.

True success encompasses personal fulfillment, growth, and making a positive impact on others.

In conclusion, the song “Milly Rock” by LAY OFF explores themes of financial struggle, the pursuit of wealth, and the importance of perseverance. Through its lyrics, the song invites listeners to reflect on their own experiences with financial hardships and serves as a reminder that success is attainable through hard work, dedication, and patience.
