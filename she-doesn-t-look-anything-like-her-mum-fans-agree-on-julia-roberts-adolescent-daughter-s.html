She Doesnt Look Anything like Her Mum: Fans Agree on Julia Roberts' Adolescent Daughters

Julia Roberts | Henry, Danny, Phinnaeus, and Hazel Moder, and Julia Roberts | Source: Getty Images

  • The eldest daughter of Hollywood star couple Julia Roberts and Danny Moder made her first-ever red carpet debut in 2021.
  • Hazel Moder, who was 16 then, accompanied her dad to the Cannes Film Festival, and fans who saw her complimented her on how pretty she's grown up to be.
  • Fans also said she looks nothing like her famous mom.

World-renowned actress Julia Roberts, 55, is a loving wife to American cinematographer Danny Moder, 54. The couple has been married for over 20 years, and their love story began in 2001 while filming a movie.

Danny Moder and Julia Roberts at the 71st Annual Golden Globe Awards in Beverly Hills, California on January 12, 2014 | Source: Getty Images

At the time, both of them were involved in other relationships, so they did not start dating right away. However, they began courting after calling off their past relationships with their previous partners and have been together ever since.

The lovebirds tied the knot on July 4, 2002. Of the love she has for her spouse and their union, the "Pretty Woman" star gushed:

"He truly, to this day, to this minute is just my favorite human. We just really, really like each other, and we just enjoy each other's company."

Danny Moder, Julia Roberts, Kelly Slater, Phinnaeus Moder, Henry Moder and Hazel Moder at the Kelly Slater, John Moore and Friends Celebrate the Launch of Outerknown event in Malibu, California on August 29, 2015 | Source: Getty Images

During the course of their marriage, the duo became doting parents of three when they welcomed their twins, Hazel and Phinnaeus Moder, and their last born, Henry Moder. As protective and loving parents, Julia and Danny do their best to typically keep their children away from the spotlight.

They even moved from Los Angeles and made San Francisco their home base, purchasing a $8.3 million home as a place to keep their kids away from public scrutiny. In juxtaposition to the usual guarded sense of privacy concerning their children, their daughter Hazel stepped into the spotlight when she attended her first red carpet event with her dad.

Julia Roberts' Daughter Made Her Red-Carpet Debut, and Fans Said the Young Girl Does Not Look Like Her Mom

In 2021, Hazel, who was 16 then, made her first ever red-carpet appearance alongside her dad, whom she supported for his film "Flag Day," at the Cannes Film Festival. The blonde beauty, who had her hair tied up in a ponytail, opted for a casual chic look, adorning a yellow button-down dress with flower lace embroidery and black Mary Janes with chunky heels.

Danny and Hazel Moder at the 74th Annual Cannes Film Festival on July 10, 2021 | Source: Getty Images

Hazel made her parents proud, especially her mother, who said her daughter carried herself well. Upon recalling the rare moment, Julia praised her daughter and joked about their back and forth over her look and assertiveness with not wanting to wear eyeliner:

"She is a one-of-a-kind, that girl...I mean, it's just sweet, just the innocence..."

Hazel and Danny Moder at the 74th Annual Cannes Film Festival on July 10, 2021 | Source: Getty Images

When fans saw pictures of Hazel on her dad's arm, many complimented the young lady on how she looked and called her pretty but noted the difference in physical appearance between Hazel and Julia. One fan commented:

"She's lovely but you'd never guess who her mum is...they really are not alike at all."

Hazel and Danny Moder at the 74th Cannes Film Festival on July 10, 2021 | Source: Getty Images

While others agreed about the difference between Hazel and Julia, some asserted that she looks more like her dad. Another person reiterated:

"She doesn't look anything like her Mum."
