Personal Trainer

Like most of the professional models, Antonina has a disciplined and grueling workout regime in place. She initially used to focus solely on Pilates. While traveling, she can’t get her usual workouts done so she hits the gym instead. She also tries to find yoga studios in the city. She is so particular about working out that she doesn’t even allow a long and a hard day to get in her way and simply heads to the gym for an hour of cardio.
