Who is Greivis Vasquez?

Greivis Vasquez is a well-known Venezuelan professional basketball player. He is currently a free agent who has played for different National Basketball Association (NBA) teams. He plays point guard as well as shooting guard position.

Greivis Vasquez: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Greivis Vasquez was born in Caracas, Venezuela on 16 January 1987. As of 2021, he is 34 years old. He is of South American ethnicity and has Venezuelan nationality.

He is the son of Ivis Rodriguez(mother) and Gregorio Vasquez(father). His birth name is Greivis Josue Vasquez Rodriguez. He has a brother named Ingerman Sanoya with whom he grew up.
