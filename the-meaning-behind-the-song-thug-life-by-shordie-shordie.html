The Meaning Behind The Song: Thug Life by Shordie Shordie

September 2024 · 3 minute read

I am listening to this song while working, and it has captured my attention with its catchy beats and intriguing lyrics. Shordie Shordie, a talented rapper, delivers a powerful message in his song “Thug Life.” Let’s dive deeper into the meaning behind the lyrics.

In the first verse, Shordie Shordie contemplates whether he should go to Texas or Las Vegas, showcasing his ambition and desire to explore new places. He emphasizes that despite his success, he remains humble and never disrespects anyone. Shordie believes he is the greatest and pulls up in the latest car, representing his elevated status.

The mention of ‘More’ refers to Baltimore, Shordie Shordie’s hometown. He talks about being on the pavement, facing police raids, and the lack of salvation for those caught up in the street life. Catching a “halo wit’ a hollow” signifies the danger and violence prevalent in his surroundings.

As the song progresses into the chorus, Shordie Shordie draws inspiration from Tupac Shakur, an iconic figure in the world of rap. He mentions Tupac on Flamingo, paying homage to the late rapper and highlighting the impact he has had on his own life and music.

The lyrics also depict a tumultuous relationship with a woman. Shordie Shordie uses codeine as a metaphor for escaping reality and possibly numbing himself from the pain. The line about the woman pretending to know him and performing intimate acts slowly suggests a lack of authenticity and shallow connections in his romantic encounters.

In the second verse, Shordie Shordie discusses the presence of fake people in his life. He questions their identities and wonders how they can lie so much. His success and growth become apparent as he acknowledges that they must be jealous of his achievements.

Throughout the song, Shordie Shordie’s introspective lyrics reflect on various aspects of his life, relationships, and personal struggles. It is evident that he has matured and gained wisdom, as he no longer tolerates disrespect or engages in meaningless interactions.

“Thug Life” is featured on Shordie Shordie’s album titled “A Life For Two,” set to be released on April 14, 2023. The song’s production credits go to Ryda, DCO Sounds, Chino Raw Beatz, and MaxxTrell. The mixing and mastering were handled by Rob Middleton, with recording engineering by Ali “A MAC” McGuire.

In conclusion, “Thug Life” by Shordie Shordie delves into the complexities of his experiences, showcasing his growth as an artist and individual. The lyrics explore themes of ambition, authenticity, and the challenges one faces in relationships and street life. This song serves as a reminder of the struggles and sacrifices many artists endure along their journey to success, and it adds to Shordie Shordie’s repertoire of thought-provoking tracks.
