See John Travolta's Grey Beard as Santa Claus in a Commercial with Samuel L Jackson

"Pulp Fiction" co-stars John Travolta and Samuel L. Jackson reunited in a hilarious Christmas commercial for Capital One where Travolta appeared with white beards as Santa.

John Travolta is one of Hollywood's A-list actors, who has graced both TV and films with his charming personality. The entertainer recently reunited with his "Pulp Fiction" co-star Samuel L. Jackson for a holiday-themed commercial.

The father-of-three uploaded the ad for Capital One, an American credit card company on his Instagram, and the footage revealed an unrecognizable Travolta dressed as Santa Claus. His outfit consisted of a long-sleeved white shirt with a gold-buttoned red vest and matching red trousers.

John Travolta speaks onstage at the premiere of Quiver Distribution's "The Fanatic" on August 22, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

The jolly look was completed with white slicked-back hair and a full beard. The commercial began with the actor going through his Christmas shopping list on his laptop when Jackson popped up on a video call to tell him about the advantages of Capital One.

Back in October, [Travolta] shared a precious throwback of daughter Ella Bleu holding her younger brother Benjamin.

The ad ended with a friendly argument between the co-stars before Santa and Mrs. Claus danced to Chuck Berry's "Run Rudolph Run." Travolta playfully captioned the post with the words:

"Still need to decide if @samuelljackson is naughty or nice."

Although the co-stars weren't replaying their roles as Vincent Vega and Jules Winnfield, fans were quick to notice that the commercial was filled with "Pulp Fiction" references. A fan stated that Travolta's shopping list consisted of a "bolo tie" which his character wore in the movie.

His hair was also slicked back in the commercial similar to Vega's style and the opening moment of the ad captured the actor drinking a milkshake with a straw which could be a reference to the "$5 milkshake" scene from the film.

The Capital One commercial wasn't the only sweet thing Travolta has posted this year. Back in October, the father-of-three shared a precious throwback of daughter Ella Bleu holding her younger brother Benjamin.

This adorable post came weeks after he uploaded a heartwarming picture of his 1991 wedding to his late wife, Kelly Preston alongside a photo of his parents' wedding in honor of her birthday.

The actress died in July after a two-year battle with breast cancer. Her last role was with her husband in the 2018 movie "Gotti," where they portrayed the lovers' Victoria and John Gotti.

However, tragedy struck once more as the 66-year-old lost his nephew, Sam Travolta Jr. in September. No information was released about the cause of death but sources have confirmed that Sam Jr. died at 52.
