Who is Kirk Cameron?

Kirk Cameron is a popular American actor.

He is popular for his acting skills in Growing Pains, Like Father Like Son, Listen To Me, and more.

As a writer, he wrote two documentaries Kirk Cameron: Connect plus Unstoppable and 13 episodes of The Way of the Master.

His birthdate is 12 Oct 1970 and he was born in Panorama City, Los Angeles, California, the US as Bruce Travis McGill.

The name of his mother was Barbara (housewife) and his father was Robert (teacher).
