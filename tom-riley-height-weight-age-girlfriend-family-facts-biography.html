Tom Riley is an English actor, producer, and director. He is best known for his portrayal of Leonardo Da Vinci in the historical fantasy drama series, Da Vinci’s Demons, and his appearances in shows like Doctor Who, Inside No. 9, The Collection, and Dark Heart. The actor has gained quite a popularity and has amassed over 50k followers on Instagram.

    Tom Riley Facts

  • Tom first got involved in drama in his hometown when he was only 4.
  • He spent most of his school years writing and directing plays and also had his own radio show at the University.
  • He worked with the Royal Court Theatre company while still studying at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art and appeared in the play, The Woman Before, shortly before graduating.
  • He also started a small theatre company, Article 19, out of his love for theatre and stage.
  • He was nominated for a Drama Desk Award for Best-Featured Actor in a Play for his performance in Tom Stoppard‘s Arcadia on Broadway in 2012.
  • In 2014, he became the winner of the Best Actor BAFTA Cymru Award for his work in the historical fantasy drama series, Da Vinci’s Demons.
