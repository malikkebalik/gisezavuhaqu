Is Cody Rigsby in a relationship, Partner, Dating History – Cody Rigsby, the charismatic fitness guru, has not only become the face of Peloton but has also waltzed into the hearts of many as a contestant on Dancing With the Stars. Beyond the workout sessions and the glitzy dance floor, Cody’s life unfolds with a compelling love story, professional journey, and an unexpected twist due to the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Cody’s Relationship with Andrés Alfaro

In 2018, Cody Rigsby found more than just success in the fitness world; he discovered love with Andrés Alfaro. The couple decided to keep their relationship under wraps for the first two years. However, a 2020 profile in Vogue and a subsequent increase in shared Instagram photos brought their love story into the limelight. Andrés, an instructor at Barry’s Bootcamp, became the not-so-secret other half of the Peloton sensation.

More Than a Fitness Instructor

Andrés Alfaro, a Barry’s Bootcamp instructor, is more than just a fitness enthusiast. Describing himself as someone who “can’t live without my Nespresso,” Andrés shares a glimpse of his personality. His guilty pleasures include sleeping in and indulging in cream cheese bagels. Friends describe him as “commanding, opinionated, and a playful sass,” adding a layer of personality to the duo’s public narrative.

Cody and Andrés on Instagram

Since going public with their relationship, Cody and Andrés share their love story through sweet moments captured on Instagram. While their captions may be brief, the pictures speak volumes, offering a glimpse into the joy they find in each other’s company.

Cody’s Journey to Fitness Fame

Cody Rigsby’s path to fame wasn’t a traditional one. Introduced to Peloton through a friend’s advice about the attractive $150 per class payment, Cody quickly realized he wanted to bring a different energy to the workout scene. Dissatisfied with the seriousness of other instructors, Cody developed a unique and lively style that resonated with Peloton users. With over 900,000 Instagram followers, Cody has become one of Peloton’s main faces, surpassing even some of the brand’s other prominent instructors.

Dancing With the Stars

Cody’s Peloton fame didn’t confine him to the exercise bike. In the latest season of Dancing With the Stars, Cody partnered with Cheryl Burke and quickly became a fan favorite. Despite being talked about as contenders for the season’s victory, a sudden obstacle threatened to derail their journey.

Cody and Cheryl’s Positive Tests

On September 26, 2021, Cheryl Burke shared on Instagram that she had tested positive for COVID-19, leading to a 10-day quarantine. Just days later, on October 1, 2021, Cody also announced his positive test, marking his second bout with the virus that year. Despite facing adversity, Cody expressed gratitude for the effectiveness of the vaccine and antibodies, highlighting his minor symptoms, including congestion, headaches, and a cough.

Cody and Cheryl Remote Journey

Undeterred by the positive tests, Cody and Cheryl decided to continue their Dancing With the Stars journey remotely. In a virtual appearance on Good Morning America, they revealed their commitment to dancing from the comfort of their own homes. Cheryl humorously mentioned turning her living room into a ballroom, emphasizing their determination to persevere through challenging circumstances.

Cody Enthusiasm and Cheryl’s Hopes

Despite the challenges, Cody maintained his excitement for the competition, especially for the Britney Spears-themed week. Cheryl expressed pride in Cody’s hard work and hoped for a swift return to the ballroom. The duo’s perseverance reflects not only their dedication to the competition but also their ability to find joy in the face of adversity.
