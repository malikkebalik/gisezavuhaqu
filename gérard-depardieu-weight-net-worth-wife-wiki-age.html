Who Is Gérard Depardieu? - Gérard Depardieu Wikipedia

Gérard Depardieu Wikipedia: - Gérard Depardieu is a French actor, known to be one of the most prolific in film history. Gérard Depardieu was born on 27 December 1948 in Châteauroux, France. Since 1967, he has completed almost 250 films, nearly entirely as a starring role. Depardieu has collaborated with over 150 filmmakers, including Jean-Luc Godard, François Truffaut, Maurice Pialat, Alain Resnais, Claude Chabrol, Ridley Scott, and Bernardo Bertolucci. He is the second highest-grossing actor in French cinema history, after only Louis de Funès. His corpus of work also includes several television programs, 18 theater plays, 16 recordings, and 9 books as of January 2022.

Depardieu has been awarded the Légion d'honneur and the Ordre National du Mérite. In January 2013, he was awarded Russian citizenship (legally adopted name in Russian: еpар савие еард, romanized: Zherar Ksavie Depardyo) and appointed Montenegrin cultural ambassador.
